Fun Problems (Perhaps Pertaining to Course Content)

Note: many of these problems are adapted from "Problems of the Day" given at the beginning of lectures by Prof. Sinclair and Prof. Song in the Fall 2019 iteration of CS 70. I enjoyed solving these problems, and hope that current students of CS 70 will find them interesting as well.

I. Two Logic Puzzles:

1. Bob is accused of murder. Bob's attorney never lies. The Judge states:"If Bob committed this crime, he didn't act alone." The Attorney responds:"That's not true!" Question: Did the attorney help Bob?

2. Is the statement "A watched child never falls asleep unless they are watched" True, False, or Undetermined?
